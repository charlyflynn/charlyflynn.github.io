music.

I am a multi-instrumentalist, composer and producer.

As a session musician, I have recorded at BBC Maida Vale Studios, Total Refreshment Centre and Brighton Road Recording Studios for artists/projects such as Dirty Nice, Orii Community and Zami Yazeed.

My compositions have found their way into podcasts and exhibitions, most recently scoring the exhibition and short film ⧉The Store by Tilly Wace and Kaia Goodenough, held at Staffordshire Street gallery in Peckham.

You can otherwise find me playing bass with my band ⧉pangaea, or perusing London's offering of gigs and jams.
